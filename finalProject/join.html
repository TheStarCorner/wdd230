Membership Descriptions

Non-Profit ($100): This tier is great for supporting the company. It is also great for helping those in need as well. In addition, you will receive an exclusive invite to our special events (Note: The higher tiers will give more options to do in the events.)

Bronze ($200): This tier gives you the same tiers as the last one, but you have more things to do at our events. For those in a different company, you can advertise your company in our website (For this tier, the amount of space of the adversitement will be small. Higher tiers will result in larger views for your advertisement.) Your training will be a great experience (Higher tiers will include more training and experiences).

Silver ($400):: For this tier (in addition to the previous tier's benefits), you will have full access to exclusive private events for the company (Note: The highest tier will have more options at those events). In addition, you will have a say for what we can do for our company and our customers (as long as it fits in our guidelines). Also, you will be in a high positon for the job (as long as you are fit for the job) if that is what you want. If not, you still have a contributing voice for the company. You will be given a great experience for learning how to work at our company.

Gold ($1,000): In addition to all previous tiers, you will have the greatest training of all of the other tiers. If you are in our company, you will be in one of the higher positions (as long as you follow the rules and guidelines). Your contributions to the company will be vital to production. You will be invited to all events, and you will be able to invite others to the events as well (as long as they follow our guidelines and standards).
